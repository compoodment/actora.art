# Music

[Music](https://actora.art/music/) is actora.art's public music player. Guests can browse and listen; signed-in visitors also get a personal Library, playlists, uploads, listening history, and sharing.

## Discover and Library

Discover contains published songs, albums, and public playlists. A signed-in visitor's Library contains their own or saved collections, while **All tracks** can switch between Library tracks and all public Music.

Albums are public. New playlists are private by default and become discoverable only when their owner makes them public.

## Playback

The player stays available while moving between Discover, collections, tracks, Now playing, Queue, and Lyrics. **Play next** and **Add to queue** work for guests as well as signed-in visitors.

Signed-in listening history powers private **Continue listening** and **Recently played** shelves. It can be cleared at any time and is never public profile or activity data.

## Add Songs

Signed-in visitors can add MP3, M4A/AAC, Ogg/Opus, or WAV files. Selected audio, cover art, and details remain in a private review until the uploader explicitly publishes or discards them. Nothing enters the public catalog before **Publish**.

Uploaders can edit or delete their own published songs. Playlist owners can manage their own playlists and change their visibility.

## Sharing and Profiles

Songs, albums, and public playlists can be shared with current friends through Messages. The message keeps a recognizable title if the Music later becomes unavailable, but its active link and playback action disappear.

A profile can show up to eight selected public songs, available albums, and public playlists. Items disappear from the showcase if they are deleted, become unavailable, or a playlist is made private.

Private playlists, unpublished reviews, Library state, and listening history stay private. Music has no public listening history, activity feed, or popularity metrics. See [social.md](social.md) and [privacy.md](privacy.md) for the wider boundaries.
